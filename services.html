[ Services ]

Every element is a decision.

Five areas of work. One approach: study the brand, find what's true, build from there.

NODE doesn't produce deliverables. It builds systems. Each service is designed to address a specific gap — and they're built to work together when a brand needs the full picture.

01

Lab Session

Before building anything, we read what's there.

The Lab Session is a diagnostic — a deep look at the brand as it exists right now. We identify where communication breaks down, what's missing, and where the real opportunity lives. Everything that follows builds on this foundation.

What we work on

  • Brand essence and how it reads across all touchpoints
  • Content pillars — clarity, consistency, alignment with goals
  • Profile audit: bio, links, highlights, overall aesthetic
  • Gap diagnosis between current presence and real potential
  • Content strategy direction
  • Concrete recommendations, ready to apply

02

Branding

Your brand is more than a logo.

It's how people describe you when you're not in the room. The reason they choose you — or don't.

We build from the inside out: starting with who you are, what you stand for, and where you're going. Then we translate that into a visual and verbal identity that is coherent, intentional, and built to hold.

What we work on

  • Brand strategy and positioning
  • Logo design and alternate versions
  • Color palette and typography system
  • Visual identity and brand applications
  • Verbal identity and brand voice
  • Brand guide
